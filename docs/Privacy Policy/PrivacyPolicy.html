Privacy Policy

At Lauren Bays Beauty, your privacy is a top priority for us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and other details submitted through appointment requests, contact forms, or newsletter signups.
  • Health Information: When relevant, we may collect health-related details you choose to share during booking or intake forms for treatment purposes.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site (collected via cookies and analytics tools).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage appointments and services
  • Respond to inquiries and communications
  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., booking software, email platforms) who help us operate our business
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies, if required by law

Cookies & Tracking

Our site may use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and improve our content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional emails at any time (via the unsubscribe link)

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
